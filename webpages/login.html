About Us

ECALSDS is an advanced e-commerce and logistics system designed to connect Direct Suppliers with consumers. Our goal is to enhance efficiency, ensure sustainable practices, and provide a seamless online shopping experience.

How the System Works
  • Step 1: Users register as either Buyer or Seller.
  • Step 2: Sellers list products, and Buyers browse the available options.
  • Step 3: Orders are placed and processed through our secure logistics system.
  • Step 4: Delivery tracking and notifications ensure smooth transactions.
  • Step 5: Customer support is available for any assistance needed.

Need further assistance? Contact our support team anytime.
